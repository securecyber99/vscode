About The Vale

The Vale of Arryn is one of the most isolated kingdoms in Westeros, protected by its mountainous terrain. It is ruled by House Arryn from The Eyrie, a massive castle built on a mountain. The Vale is known for its natural defenses and its knights who ride the mountains. The Arryns have a strong tradition of honor and are known for producing great leaders. The region is relatively safe from external threats due to its geography.

Geography & Culture

The Vale is dominated by mountains and is nearly impossible to invade due to its natural defenses. The Eyrie sits so high that few dare approach it. The Vale is home to proud knights and followers of the Faith of the Seven. The region is fertile in the valleys and known for producing skilled warriors. The people of the Vale take pride in their chivalry and honor.

Notable Events

The Vale remained relatively neutral during much of the conflict in the Six Kingdoms. However, it became a key player when Littlefinger orchestrated the Vale into supporting Robb Stark and later assisting in the Battle of the Bastards. The Vale's knights played crucial roles in several major battles, and the kingdom's isolation kept it safe from the worst ravages of the wars.
