About The Reach

The Reach is the most fertile and agriculturally rich region of the Six Kingdoms. It is ruled by House Tyrell from their gorgeous seat at Highgarden. The Reach is known for its prosperity, beauty, and refined culture. House Tyrell is renowned for its strategic marriages and political maneuvering. The region is the breadbasket of Westeros, supplying food to much of the continent. The Tyrells are known for their grace, cunning, and political acumen.

Geography & Culture

The Reach is characterized by lush forests, fertile fields, and beautiful rivers. Highgarden is famous for its magnificent gardens and architecture. The region is less military focused than others, relying instead on economic power and political alliances. The Reach follows the Faith of the Seven and is known for courtly manners and refined tastes. The people value beauty, culture, and prosperity, making the Reach the wealthiest and most developed kingdom.

Notable Events

The Reach remained largely neutral during much of the War of the Five Kings before eventually supporting the Lannisters. House Tyrell used marriage alliances with the Baratheons and later the Lannisters to increase their power. Margaery Tyrell's marriages to multiple kings demonstrated the family's political ambitions. The Reach's wealth and food supply made it crucial to all factions. The destruction of the Great Sept of Baelor had major implications for the Tyrell family's influence.
