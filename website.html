The Seven Kingdoms & Their Rulers

The Seven Kingdoms of Westeros are ruled by different houses, each with their own sigils, histories, and claims to power. Below are the major kingdoms and their most notable rulers throughout the series.

The North

🐺
Capital: Winterfell
Ruler: House Stark
Key Rulers: Jon Snow, Sansa Stark, Ned Stark
"Winter is Coming"

The Vale of Arryn

🦅
Capital: The Eyrie
Ruler: House Arryn
Key Rulers: Jon Arryn, Robin Arryn
"As High as Honor"

The Riverlands

🐟
Capital: Riverrun
Ruler: House Tully
Key Rulers: Hoster Tully, Edmure Tully
"Family, Duty, Honor"

The Westerlands

🦁
Capital: Casterly Rock
Ruler: House Lannister
Key Rulers: Tywin Lannister, Cersei Lannister
"Hear Me Roar"

The Reach

🌹
Capital: Highgarden
Ruler: House Tyrell
Key Rulers: Mace Tyrell, Olenna Tyrell
"We Are the Fury"

The Stormlands

⚡
Capital: Storm's End
Ruler: House Baratheon
Key Rulers: Robert Baratheon, Stannis Baratheon
"Ours is the Fury"

The Crownlands

👑
Capital: King's Landing
Ruler: House Targaryen/Baratheon
Key Rulers: Daenerys Targaryen, Aerys II Targaryen
"Fire and Blood"

Dorne

🐉
Capital: Sunspear
Ruler: House Martell
Key Rulers: Doran Martell, Oberyn Martell
"Unbowed, Unbent, Unbroken"
