About The Westerlands

The Westerlands is one of the wealthiest kingdoms in Westeros, known for its vast gold mines. It is ruled by House Lannister from their fortress, Casterly Rock, built into a mountain. The Lannisters are known for their wealth, ambition, and cunning. The region is prosperous and powerful, allowing House Lannister to wield significant influence across the Six Kingdoms. The Westerlands pride themselves on strength and the acquisition of power and wealth.

Geography & Culture

The Westerlands are filled with hills and mountains rich in gold and other valuable resources. Casterly Rock is one of the most impregnable fortresses in Westeros. The region is relatively isolated from the conflicts in the east due to its location. The people are known for their pride and their loyalty to House Lannister. The Westerlands are prosperous and their inhabitants enjoy wealth and comfort due to the region's resources.

Notable Events

The Westerlands played a crucial role in the War of the Five Kings, with Tywin Lannister leading armies against the North. Casterly Rock was the power base from which the Lannisters wielded enormous political influence. The wealth of the Westerlands funded the Lannister ambitions across all of Westeros. The kingdom remained relatively stable throughout much of the conflict, allowing House Lannister to position itself as keepers of order and stability.
