About The Crownlands

The Crownlands is the seat of royal power and the most important region politically. It is ruled by whoever sits on the Iron Throne and is home to King's Landing, the capital of the Six Kingdoms. The Crownlands are relatively small but incredibly significant as they contain the heart of government and power. The region is dominated by its capital and its royal fortress, the Red Keep. The Crownlands were originally directly ruled by House Targaryen but came under different rulers following the rebellion.

Geography & Culture

King's Landing is built on Blackwater Bay and is the largest city in Westeros. The Red Keep dominates the skyline with its distinctive red stone towers. The Crownlands are coastal and benefit from trade and commerce through the port. The Great Sept of Baelor was the main place of worship for the Faith of the Seven. The Crownlands are the center of power, intrigue, and political maneuvering, where the most crucial decisions affecting the kingdom are made.

Notable Events

The Crownlands were the site of Robert's Rebellion against the Targaryen dynasty. King's Landing saw immense political intrigue, murder, and conspiracy throughout the series. The destruction of the Great Sept of Baelor was one of the most shocking events, killing hundreds. Multiple rulers occupied the Iron Throne throughout the series, each bringing their own vision to the kingdom. The Crownlands ultimately came under the control of Daenerys Targaryen, bringing the Targaryen name back to power.
