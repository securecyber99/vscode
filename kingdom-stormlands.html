About The Stormlands

The Stormlands is a region known for its treacherous weather and fierce storms. It is ruled by House Baratheon from their fortress at Storm's End, one of the oldest castles in Westeros. The landslanders are hardy, brave, and skilled knights. House Baratheon is known for its strength, pride, and martial prowess. The region has produced powerful leaders including the legendary Aegon the Conqueror during his own conquest. The Stormlands are prosperous and militarily powerful.

Geography & Culture

The Stormlands are characterized by harsh weather, violent storms, and fierce winds. Storm's End stands on a rocky peninsula and has never been conquered by siege, defended by an impregnable position. The region is warmer than the North but more tempestuous than other southern kingdoms. The people are known for their bravery and military tradition. The Stormlands follow the Faith of the Seven and value chivalry and martial honor.

Notable Events

Robert Baratheon's rebellion against the Targyaens began in the Stormlands, and he became the first king after the dynasty's fall. The Stormlands provided military strength during the War of the Five Kings through Stannis's efforts. Storm's End endured multiple sieges and remained a symbol of House Baratheon's power. The succession of the Stormlands became complicated after Robert's death, with multiple Baratheon branches claiming the throne and title.
